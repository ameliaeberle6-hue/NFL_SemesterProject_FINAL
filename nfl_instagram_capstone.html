Why this topic?

Social media has fundamentally changed how fans engage with sports teams. Instagram follower counts aren't just vanity metrics — they reflect cultural moments, viral stories, and the expanding global reach of the NFL.

We wanted to understand: do championship runs drive Instagram growth? Or do off-field stories — like a celebrity relationship — matter just as much? Some of the most dramatic follower spikes in our data have nothing to do with the win/loss record.

Taylor Swift attending Kansas City Chiefs games in 2023 is the most striking example, but Simone Biles marrying Jonathan Owens, Brady joining Tampa Bay, and Mahomes' MVP campaigns each tell their own story.
